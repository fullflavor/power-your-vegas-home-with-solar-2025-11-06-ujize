Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about solar energy and our installation process.

The amount you save depends on several factors including your current electricity usage, roof size, sun exposure, and local utility rates. On average, Las Vegas homeowners save 50-90% on their electricity bills. Most customers save $150-300+ per month. We provide a detailed, personalized quote that shows your projected savings over 10, 20, and 25 years. Many of our customers see a complete return on their investment within 5-8 years, then enjoy free electricity for the remaining warranty period.

Solar panels do produce electricity on cloudy days, though at a reduced rate—typically 10-25% of their peak capacity. At night, your system doesn't produce electricity, but you're covered in two ways: if you have a battery backup system, it stores excess daytime energy for nighttime use. Without batteries, you remain connected to the grid and can draw power as needed. Las Vegas's average of 310 sunny days per year means your system will generate substantial power throughout the year. Our monitoring system allows you to track your production in real-time.

We offer multiple financing options to fit your budget: purchase with cash (best for maximizing savings), solar loans (no money down, own your system immediately), solar leases (lowest upfront cost), and power purchase agreements (pay only for the energy produced). We also help you take advantage of the federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC), which currently allows you to deduct 30% of your installation costs from your federal taxes. Many customers combine these incentives to reduce their net cost significantly. Our financing specialists will work with you to find the best option for your situation.

The actual installation typically takes 1-3 days depending on your system size and roof complexity. However, the complete process from initial consultation to activation usually takes 30-45 days. This includes site assessment, system design, permitting (2-3 weeks), scheduling, installation, and final inspections. Our team handles all the paperwork with the city and utility company. We'll keep you informed at every step and provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation. Once your system is activated, you'll start generating clean energy immediately.

All our systems come with comprehensive warranties: 25-year performance warranty on panels (guaranteeing 80%+ output), 10-year warranty on inverters and equipment, and 10-year workmanship warranty on installation. Solar systems require minimal maintenance—occasional cleaning and inspections are all that's needed. We offer optional maintenance plans that include annual inspections, cleaning, and performance monitoring. Our monitoring system alerts us to any issues, and our support team can often resolve problems remotely. Most customers find their systems operate flawlessly for 25+ years with virtually no maintenance costs.
